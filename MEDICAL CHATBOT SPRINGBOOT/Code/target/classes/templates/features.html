Project Features

🩺

AI Disease Prediction

Our core feature guides you from symptoms to a potential diagnosis. Simply enter up to five symptoms, and our AI will initiate a guided conversation to provide a list of possible illnesses, safe generic medicines, and lifestyle advice.

📄

Intelligent Prescription Analysis

Upload a PDF of your prescription, and our advanced RAG-supported AI will analyze it. It cross-references the medicines with your patient profile to check for potential allergic reactions, dangerous drug interactions, and unsafe dosages.

👤

Personalized Patient Profile

Easily create and update your personal health profile. By providing your age, known allergies, and medical history, you enable the AI to give more personalized and safer recommendations, tailored specifically to your health conditions.

💾

Downloadable PDF Reports

After receiving a disease prediction or a prescription analysis, you have the option to download the complete report as a PDF. This allows you to keep a personal record or share the AI's findings with a healthcare professional.

Project Workflow

Step 1

Profile Setup

Navigate to the "Patient Profile" section in the assistant dashboard to enter your age, allergies, and medical history. This is crucial for personalized advice.

Step 2

Choose Your Action

Select either "Predict Disease" to diagnose new symptoms or "Analyze Prescription" to check an existing prescription from the dashboard.

Step 3

Interact with the AI

If predicting a disease, provide your symptoms and answer the AI's follow-up questions. If analyzing a prescription, upload the PDF document.

Step 4

Receive and Download Report

The AI will display the full report in the chat window. A download button will appear, allowing you to save the results as a PDF for your records.
